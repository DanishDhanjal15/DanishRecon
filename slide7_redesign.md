# Target Audience and Engagement

## 1. Security & SOC Analysts (Defensive)
* **The Value:** A unified *"single pane of glass."* Translates isolated logs into visual attack graphs to clearly show how misconfigurations impact critical internal assets.

## 2. Penetration Testers & Red Teams (Offensive)
* **The Value:** Accelerates reconnaissance. Combines OSINT, port scanning, and exploit mapping to reveal immediate attack vectors without endless terminal jockeying.

## 3. Auditors & Compliance Officers 
* **The Value:** Eliminates manual mapping. Directly translates raw technical data (e.g., outdated cipher suites) into actionable, executive-ready PCI-DSS and NIST reports.

## 4. Small/Medium Enterprises (SMEs)
* **The Value:** Democratized Security. Organizations without the $10,000+ budget for enterprise scanners receive a deeply capable, accessible tool for identifying perimeter blind spots.
